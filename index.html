How Verification Works

The VANTAGE trust engine

🔍 The Two-Step Verification Process

1

AI Measurement

Our AI analyzes your Instagram video frame-by-frame. It detects takeoff position, peak jump height, and uses the basketball rim (10ft) as a reference to calculate your exact vertical leap. Sprint times are measured using frame timing and known court distances.

AI Confidence Score: 0-100% based on video quality, rim visibility, and camera angle.

2

Community Confirmation

Other users watch your video and review the AI's measurement. If they agree it's accurate, they click "Confirm." Each metric needs 2-3 confirmations (depending on AI confidence) to become fully verified.

High confidence (90%+) = auto-verified. Medium (70-89%) = needs 2 confirmations. Low (below 70%) = needs 3 confirmations.

3

Verified Badge

Once enough community members confirm your metrics, you receive a green "Verified" badge. Your verified profile becomes searchable and visible to scouts.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What makes VANTAGE different from other platforms?

Unlike Hudl or recruiting platforms that rely on self-reported stats, VANTAGE uses AI + community verification to ensure every metric is accurate. Scouts can trust what they see.

Can I challenge a verification I think is wrong?

Yes. Click "Challenge" on any athlete's profile. The metric will be re-reviewed by our team and the community.

How do I earn a higher trust score?

Consistently confirm accurate metrics and maintain a daily verification streak. Higher trust scores make you a "Trusted Verifier" - your confirmations carry more weight.

Is VANTAGE really free for athletes?

Yes. Athletes never pay. Scouts pay $500/year for full database access, advanced filters, and export tools.
